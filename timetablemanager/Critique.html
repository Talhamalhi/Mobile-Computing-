These are the main related apps that are already in use in market. Some of them are available on both google play store and iOS app store and some are only available on google play store. The use of the apps is somewhat similar. All are timetable manager apps with almost the same target audience.

All of them have a sign-up screen which work fine. The apps explain themselves well during the first 2-3 minutes of use. The apps were quite easy to use. Overall, it was a welcoming and user-friendly feel while exploring the app. Almost all these apps deliver to the expectations of the user while some improvements could be done like matching the background light of the device. An average of 30 minutes was spent on each app.
